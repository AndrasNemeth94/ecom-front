Our story

Our business has started at 2005, in the golden age of skateboarding and other extreme sports. Naturally, we were obsessed with various types of them. As a teenager it wasn't about the sport only though, we also admired the different kind of outfits sold by brands you're sure familiar with, like Vans or Volcom. Throughout the years we developed a deep interest in fashion in general, so we had the idea, that we should make a business out of it. Thats how it turned out to be the crown of a strong friendship since the 90's.

The shop

We started out our business as a group of friends intersted in street fashion. Our love towards unique items were always a huge drive for us, and after a short period of time we decided to make a business out of it. We are all for the people who think that they want more than the average collection of a mall or other commerce shops.

Delivery details

We are using the services of GLS for ensure your package will be delivered just in time. After your submitted your order to us, our webshop processes it -which takes the maximum of one day- and after that we give your package to the courier on the very day you've ordered it. There might be cases where the order has been submitted to us, but our stock is short on the particular item, in this case we will always contact you on an instant and give you the opportunity to take back your order, or choose to wait until we refill our stock. If you've chosen home delivery you will get your package in the interval of 1-3 workdays. You can also write a note, and add it to the order, we also give this information to the courier so they can contact you for further details on the time and place of the delivery. If you have chosen personal purchase, you can contact us through one of the platforms embedded in the footer of our webpage -facebook/instagram/email/twitter- and notify us about when could you take your package in person at the shop.

About delivery fee: We charge 10$ for every home delivery, personal purchases go without any plus fees naturally.
